Welcome to IndabaX 2025!

IndabaX 2025,
Maseno Uni 18th-20thJune Shaping AI

IndabaX Kenya is the Kenyan chapter of the Deep Learning Indaba, an initiative that promotes the growth and advancement of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML) across Africa. Rooted in the values of community and knowledge-sharing, IndabaX Kenya brings together students, researchers, academics, tech professionals and entrepreneurs to explore and engage with AI innovations shaping the country.

This vibrant three-day event is filled with keynote talks, panel discussions, workshops, showcases and networking opportunities. Whether you're a student curious about AI or a professional shaping its future, IndabaX Kenya offers a space to connect, learn and grow.
